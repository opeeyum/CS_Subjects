Question: Design the following functions suing minmum number of 2 input NAND gates.

1. f = $\overline A$
2. f = A . B
3. f = A + B
4. f = A . B + C . D
5. f = A + B . C
6. f = A + $\overline B$ . C
7. f = A . B + C . D + E . F
8. f = A . B + B . C + C . D + D . A 

Answers:

1. 1
2. 2
3. 3
4. 3
5. 3
6. 4
7. 6
8. 6
